# Create Your First Agent

In this lesson, you'll create your first AI agent in Langdock. Agents are customizable AI assistants that can be tailored to specific tasks, departments, or workflows.

<Callout type="info">
  An agent in Langdock is like having a specialized team member — you give it a name, a purpose, and the knowledge it needs to do its job.
</Callout>

## What you'll learn

- How to create a new agent
- How to add a knowledge folder to your agent
- Best practices for naming and configuring agents

## Let's get started

<Step number={1}>
  In your Langdock workspace (on the right), navigate to the **Agents** section using the sidebar.
</Step>

<Step number={2}>
  Click the **New Agent** button. Give your agent a descriptive name — for example, "Customer Support Helper" or "Onboarding Guide".
</Step>

<Callout type="tip">
  Good agent names describe their purpose. Instead of "My Agent", try something like "Sales Playbook Assistant" or "HR Policy Expert".
</Callout>

<Step number={3}>
  Once your agent is created, open its settings. Look for the **Knowledge** section and add a new knowledge folder. This is where you'll upload documents that your agent can reference.
</Step>
